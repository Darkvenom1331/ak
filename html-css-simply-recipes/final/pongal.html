Pongal

Ingredients:

1.1 cup rice
2.1/2 cup split moong dal
3.3 cups water 4.1/2 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
5.1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds\
6.1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing) 7.1 inch ginger, grated 8.1-2 green chilies, chopped 9.10-12 curry leaves 10.2 tablespoons ghee 11.Salt, to taste
\

instructions

Instructions:

Rinse the rice and moong dal separately in water, and soak them in enough water for 10-15 minutes..
Drain the water from the soaked rice and dal, and transfer them to a pressure cooker.
3.Add 3 cups of water, and pressure cook the rice and dal for 3-4 whistles or until they are cooked and soft. 4.Once the pressure releases, open the cooker and mash the cooked rice and dal using a wooden spoon or masher. Keep aside. 5.In a small pan, heat the ghee and add the peppercorns and cumin seeds. Let them splutter for a few seconds. 6.Add the grated ginger, chopped green chilies, and curry leaves to the pan, and saut√© for a minute. 7.Add the asafoetida to the pan, and mix well. 8.Pour this tempering over the mashed rice and dal, and mix well. 9.Add salt to taste, and mix again. 10.Serve hot with coconut chutney, sambar, or any other side dish of your choice. 11.Enjoy your delicious Pongal!
