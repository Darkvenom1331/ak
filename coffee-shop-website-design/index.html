about us why choose us

what's make our coffee special!

Welcome to Indian Coffee Shop, where we serve the best coffee from India! Our coffee is sourced from the finest farms in the country, and we take great pride in ensuring that every cup we serve is of the highest quality.

We are a family-owned business that has been serving coffee for over 20 years. Our passion for coffee started when we were kids, watching our grandfather roast coffee beans in the backyard. We grew up learning the art of coffee-making and now, as adults, we are proud to continue the tradition.

At Indian Coffee Shop, we believe that coffee should be enjoyed slowly, savoring every sip. We offer a range of brewing methods to cater to every taste, from the classic drip coffee to the more exotic pour-over and French press. We also offer a variety of espresso-based drinks, including lattes, cappuccinos, and mochas.

Our coffee shop is a warm and inviting space where you can relax and enjoy your coffee in peace. We take pride in our friendly and knowledgeable staff, who are always happy to help you find the perfect brew. We hope to see you soon at Indian Coffee Shop!

our menu popular menu

Kaapi

Kaapi is a traditional South Indian coffee that is made by brewing finely ground coffee beans with boiling water and adding milk and sugar. It is typically served in a tumbler and a davara (a small stainless steel cup) and poured back and forth to create a frothy layer on top.

Cost 10

Filter Coffee


Filter Coffee is a popular South Indian coffee that is made by brewing a mixture of coffee and chicory powder with hot water using a traditional South Indian filter. The brewed coffee is then mixed with hot milk and sugar to taste. The coffee has a unique aroma and flavor that sets it apart from other coffee varieties.

Cost 20

Mysore Coffee

Mysore Coffee is a popular South Indian coffee variety that is made by blending coffee powder with chicory powder and roasting it until it turns dark brown. The roasted mixture is then brewed with hot water using a traditional South Indian filter. The coffee is served hot and strong, and is typically sweetened with sugar and mixed with hot milk.

Cost
15

Monsooned Malabar Coffeee

Monsooned Malabar coffee is a unique Indian coffee variety that is known for its distinctively bold and earthy flavor. The coffee beans are grown in the Western Ghats region of India and are exposed to the monsoon winds, which causes them to absorb moisture and swell in size. The beans are then roasted to bring out their rich flavor and aroma.

Cost
20

Araku Valley Coffee

Araku Valley coffee is a specialty Indian coffee variety that is grown in the Araku Valley region of Andhra Pradesh. The coffee is grown at high altitudes and is known for its unique flavor profile, which includes notes of chocolate, caramel, and spices.

Cost
17

Chikmagalur Coffe

Chikmagalur coffee is a premium Indian coffee variety that is grown in the Chikmagalur district of Karnataka. It is known for its rich, full-bodied flavor and distinct aroma. The coffee beans are grown at high altitudes and are shade-grown, which helps to give them their unique taste.

Cost
30
